Hospitality Manager

Company: Macaulay Gidado

Location: Remote

Job Type: Full Time

We're looking for an experienced and dedicated Hospitality Manager to oversee and manage our vacation rentals and other properties within Africa. The ideal candidate will be in charge of maintaining a high level of guest satisfaction and monitoring property performance. This position demands a strong emphasis on operational excellence, guest interactions, and property management.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage daily operations of vacation rentals, ensuring they are guest-ready.
  • Collaborate with housekeepers, maintenance staff, and guest service representatives.
  • Ensure exceptional guest experiences by promptly resolving inquiries.
  • Oversee reservation systems, managing bookings, availability, and pricing.
  • Develop and manage budgets for each property.
  • Prepare reports on property performance, guest satisfaction, and financial results.

Qualifications:

  • Minimum of 2-3 years of experience in hospitality management.
  • Familiarity with property management software and reservation systems.
